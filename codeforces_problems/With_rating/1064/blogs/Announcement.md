# Announcement

Hi everybody,

This Sunday there will be a 16th [Moscow Team Olympiad](https://codeforces.com/http://olympiads.ru/team), high school students competition based in Moscow that is an elimination contest for All-Russian Team Olympiad. This contest is prepared by Moscow Olympiad Scientific Committee that you may know by Moscow Open Olympiad, Moscow Olympiad for Young Students and Metropolises Olympiad (rounds [327](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/21185), [342](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/23309), [345](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/43615), [376](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/47769), [401](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/50613), [433](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/54350), [441](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/55201), [466](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/57981), [469](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/58229), [507](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/61638)).

Round will be held at [10:05 UTC on Sunday](https://codeforces.com/https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/fixedtime.html?msg=Codeforces+516+%28Div.+1+%2B+Div.+2%29&iso=20181014T1005&p1=%3A) and will last for 2 hours. Each division will have 6 problems.

Problems are prepared [vintage_Vlad_Makeev](https://codeforces.com/profile/vintage_Vlad_Makeev "International Master vintage_Vlad_Makeev"), [Glebodin](https://codeforces.com/profile/Glebodin "Candidate Master Glebodin"), [Andreikkaa](https://codeforces.com/profile/Andreikkaa "Master Andreikkaa"), [qoo2p5](https://codeforces.com/profile/qoo2p5 "Grandmaster qoo2p5"), [mingaleg](https://codeforces.com/profile/mingaleg "Candidate Master mingaleg"), [Flyrise](https://codeforces.com/profile/Flyrise "Master Flyrise"), [cdkrot](https://codeforces.com/profile/cdkrot "Grandmaster cdkrot"), [achulkov2](https://codeforces.com/profile/achulkov2 "Expert achulkov2"), [grphil](https://codeforces.com/profile/grphil "Grandmaster grphil"), [Sehnsucht](https://codeforces.com/profile/Sehnsucht "Candidate Master Sehnsucht"), [Aphanasiy](https://codeforces.com/profile/Aphanasiy "Expert Aphanasiy"), [Sender](https://codeforces.com/profile/Sender "Expert Sender"), [DebNatkh](https://codeforces.com/profile/DebNatkh "Candidate Master DebNatkh"), [GreenGrape](https://codeforces.com/profile/GreenGrape "Candidate Master GreenGrape") under my supervision with great help of [GlebsHP](https://codeforces.com/profile/GlebsHP "International Grandmaster GlebsHP"), [meshanya](https://codeforces.com/profile/meshanya "Grandmaster meshanya"), [Endagorion](https://codeforces.com/profile/Endagorion "International Grandmaster Endagorion"), [Zlobober](https://codeforces.com/profile/Zlobober "International Grandmaster Zlobober") and Helen Andreeva.

Thanks to [cdkrot](https://codeforces.com/profile/cdkrot "Grandmaster cdkrot") for the round coordination and statement translation, and also thanks for [MikeMirzayanov](https://codeforces.com/profile/MikeMirzayanov "Headquarters, MikeMirzayanov") for systems codeforces and polygon, which was used to prepare problems of this olympiad.

Good luck everybody!
