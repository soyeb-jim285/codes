# Announcement

Hi!

Tomorrow, at [Thursday, February 7, 2019 at 19:35UTC+6](https://codeforces.com/https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/fixedtime.html?day=7&month=2&year=2019&hour=16&min=35&sec=0&p1=166) we will host [Codeforces Global Round 1](https://codeforces.com/contest/1110 "Codeforces Global Round 1").

It is the first round of a new series of [Codeforces Global Rounds](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/65002). The rounds are open for everybody, the rating will be updated for everybody.

The prizes for this round:

 * 30 best participants get a t-shirt.
* 20 t-shirts are randomly distributed among those with ranks between 31 and 500, inclusive.

The prizes for the 6-round series in 2019:

 * In each round top-100 participants get points according to the [table](https://codeforces.com/https://pastebin.com/QT5sXEaT).
* The final result for each participant is equal to the sum of points he gets in the four rounds he placed the highest.
* The best 20 participants over all series get sweatshirts and place certificates.

The problems of this round were developed by a team of authors: [_h_](https://codeforces.com/profile/_h_ "International Grandmaster _h_"), [simonlindholm](https://codeforces.com/profile/simonlindholm "International Grandmaster simonlindholm"), [grphil](https://codeforces.com/profile/grphil "International Master grphil"), [vintage_Vlad_Makeev](https://codeforces.com/profile/vintage_Vlad_Makeev "Master vintage_Vlad_Makeev"), [GreenGrape](https://codeforces.com/profile/GreenGrape "Master GreenGrape"), [budalnik](https://codeforces.com/profile/budalnik "Grandmaster budalnik"), [cdkrot](https://codeforces.com/profile/cdkrot "Grandmaster cdkrot") and me. Thanks [arsijo](https://codeforces.com/profile/arsijo "International Grandmaster arsijo") and [cdkrot](https://codeforces.com/profile/cdkrot "Grandmaster cdkrot") for their help in the round's coordination, and also [majk](https://codeforces.com/profile/majk "International Grandmaster majk"), [pashka](https://codeforces.com/profile/pashka "Grandmaster pashka"), [Jeel_Vaishnav](https://codeforces.com/profile/Jeel_Vaishnav "Master Jeel_Vaishnav"), [Ashishgup](https://codeforces.com/profile/Ashishgup "Master Ashishgup") and [Jatana](https://codeforces.com/profile/Jatana "Master Jatana") for testing the round!
