# Announcement

Hi everybody,

This Sunday there will be a [XVII Moscow Team Olympiad](https://codeforces.com/http://olympiads.ru/team), high school students competition based in Moscow that is an elimination contest for All-Russian Team Olympiad. This contest is prepared by Moscow Olympiad Scientific Committee that you may know by Moscow Open Olympiad, Moscow Olympiad for Young Students and Metropolises Olympiad (rounds [327](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/21185), [342](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/23309), [345](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/43615), [376](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/47769), [401](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/50613), [433](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/54350), [441](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/55201), [466](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/57981), [469](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/58229), [507](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/61638), [516](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/62440), [541](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/65433), [545](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/65768), [567](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/67703), [583](https://codeforces.com/blog/entry/69538)).

Round will be held at [Sunday, October 20, 2019 at 15:05UTC+6](https://codeforces.com/https://www.timeanddate.com/worldclock/fixedtime.html?day=20&month=10&year=2019&hour=12&min=5&sec=0&p1=166) and will last for 2 hours. Each division will have 6 problems.

Problems are prepared by [wrg0ababd](https://codeforces.com/profile/wrg0ababd "Expert wrg0ababd"), [vintage_Vlad_Makeev](https://codeforces.com/profile/vintage_Vlad_Makeev "International Grandmaster vintage_Vlad_Makeev"), [DebNatkh](https://codeforces.com/profile/DebNatkh "Master DebNatkh"), [grphil](https://codeforces.com/profile/grphil "Master grphil"), [voidmax](https://codeforces.com/profile/voidmax "Grandmaster voidmax"), [vintage_Vlad_Makeev](https://codeforces.com/profile/vintage_Vlad_Makeev "International Grandmaster vintage_Vlad_Makeev"), [volcolac](https://codeforces.com/profile/volcolac "Expert volcolac"), [Sehnsucht](https://codeforces.com/profile/Sehnsucht "Candidate Master Sehnsucht"), [isaf27](https://codeforces.com/profile/isaf27 "International Grandmaster isaf27"), [cdkrot](https://codeforces.com/profile/cdkrot "Grandmaster cdkrot"), [Flyrise](https://codeforces.com/profile/Flyrise "Master Flyrise"), [budalnik](https://codeforces.com/profile/budalnik "International Grandmaster budalnik"), [platypus179](https://codeforces.com/profile/platypus179 "International Master platypus179"), [Endagorion](https://codeforces.com/profile/Endagorion "Legendary Grandmaster Endagorion") under my supervision with great help of [GlebsHP](https://codeforces.com/profile/GlebsHP "International Grandmaster GlebsHP"), [meshanya](https://codeforces.com/profile/meshanya "Grandmaster meshanya"), [Endagorion](https://codeforces.com/profile/Endagorion "Legendary Grandmaster Endagorion"), [Zlobober](https://codeforces.com/profile/Zlobober "International Grandmaster Zlobober") and Helen Andreeva.
